Galatz and Kan 11. Collaborated with Billy Harper, Reggie Workman, James

LONG BIO
Yoav Shlomov, a Tel Aviv native, boasts a prolific jazz guitar career
spanning many years. His musical journey has been marked by collaborations
with esteemed artists such as James Francies, Shlomo Gronich, Eli Magen,
Tom Oren, Adi Rennert, Yonatan Daskal, Jenny Penkin, Ofri Nehemya, and
Tomer Bar.

He has shared stages with Larry Goldings, Omer Avital, and Stacy Dillard,
and played iconic venues worldwide — from Smalls NYC and FatCat to the Red
Sea Jazz Festival, Beit Haamudim, and Jazz Kissa. He has been featured on
mainstream radio stations including 88fm, Galatz, KZ Radio, and Kan 11.

Beyond the stage, Yoav composes and produces original scores for film and
picture, with a released catalog featured across sync and streaming
platforms. His profound passion for improvisation and his dedication to his
craft have solidified his status as a prominent figure in the global jazz
scene. His limitless and genre-less tunes make his music unique to any
listener.
